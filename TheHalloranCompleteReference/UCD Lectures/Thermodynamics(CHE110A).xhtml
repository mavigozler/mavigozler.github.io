Boyle's Law

The experimental observation for real gases in which mass is not altered in the system under study is: (PV )P→0= k

Note that this relationship occurs as the pressure of the gas is very low (i.e. P → 0). Gases are said to behave ideally, so thus for any ideal gas, the relationship PV=k

Boyle's law also holds for ice-water and boiling water baths as well, but the expression of the law will be modified for these phases.
