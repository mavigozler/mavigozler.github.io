Drug Analysis

Several prescription drugs and natural substances have significant effects on the liver, particularly cytochromes causing the biotransformation of these drugs to metabolize them (for the purposes of detoxification). Three particular cytochromes, CYP2D6, CYP2C9, and CYP2C19, are of interest.

The drugs are metabolized by the cytochrome P450 enzymes shown. Some drugs appear more than once in the three tables.
