  • Iterative Summarization.
    I discovered—much too late in life unfortunately for my own good—that the most effective way of retaining knowledge in rote memory is a process of active learning. Passive learning involves just reading the material to be learned. Active learning involves being forced to repeat what you learned. This document discusses an enhancement to that.
